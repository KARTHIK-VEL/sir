An implementation specialist first — someone who has spent over a decade making sure AI research doesn't stay on a whiteboard, but ends up running on a GPU in a working lab.

Currently an Associate Professor at Paavai Institutions and Head of Product Development (AI) at Edsols Innovations, Dr. Purusothaman has spent eleven years moving between classroom and industry floor — designing curriculum, configuring NVIDIA GPU clusters, and mentoring the teams that go on to win national hackathons.

As an NVIDIA Deep Learning Institute Ambassador, he has carried DLI training programs to more than fifteen institutions, equipping labs with edge AI kits and turning faculty into AI practitioners rather than just AI lecturers — the throughline of nearly everything in his work.
